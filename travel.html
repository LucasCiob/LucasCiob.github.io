Travel

I travel a lot, and I am grateful that I am fortunate enough to travel a lot. The best places I have been are Colorado, Hawaii, and Florida. Firstly, Colorado, I really like Colorado because of the great snow conditions, extensive trail networks, and great alpine scenery. I also like it because of the steep black diamond runs, the powder-filled bowls, and the groomed slopes. To continue, I also like Florida because of the warmth and beautiful sandy beaches and turquoise waters. I also, like going there with friends to have a great time in the warm water. I don't only like the beaches though, I also like big cities like Miami, Orlando, and Key West because of the entertainment, different food, and lively atmosphere. Lastly, Hawaii, I love Hawaii because of the different sand colors of beautiful beaches like the golden sands beaches and the black sand beaches. I also really like the water in Hawaii. It is just like Frorida but way better. Lastly, I like the great seafood, and the tall volcanoes surrounded by a rainforest landscape.
